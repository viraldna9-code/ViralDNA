## THE VIRAL DNA PROBLEM

1. SCORING IS BROKEN: Current system requires "Andhra" or "Telangana" in the headline.
   But top videos cover petrol prices, political fights, Tollywood — topics that
   don't always name the state explicitly. We miss 95% of relevant stories.

2. CONTENT IS TOO NICHE: "Andhra's Rich Villagers: No Work for 7 Years" is interesting
   journalism but NOT a growth driver. Nobody searches for that.

3. NO CELEBRITY/POLITICAL ANGLE: Top channels ride every Pawan Kalyan, NTR, CM Revanth,
   Koratala Siva wave. We have zero such content.

4. ONLY 3 VIDEOS: Even N NEWS CHANNEL has 4,264 videos. YouTube's algorithm cannot
   recommend a channel with 3 videos. The algorithm needs DATA — more videos = more
   chances to be recommended.

5. NO CONSISTENCY: No uploads for days. YouTube deprioritizes inactive channels.

## THE PLAN: 3 Growth Phases

### PHASE 1: Foundation (Week 1-2) — "Get on the Map"
Goal: 50+ videos, establish consistency signal to YouTube

CONTENT MIX:
- 1 main video/day (15-20 min news bulletin covering AP+Telangana+National headlines)
- 2 shorts/day (30-60 sec — top headline of the day, dramatic moment)

TOPIC FORMULA (in priority order):
1. AP Political drama (CM Revanth, NTR, Chandrababu, Pawan Kalyan statements)
2. Telangana Political drama (KCR, CM Revanth, local conflicts)
3. Tollywood news (movie updates, actor controversies, box office)
4. National news affecting Telugu people (petrol, taxes, exam results, jobs)
5. Telugu people achievement stories (US, UK, Australia success stories)

SCORING FIX: Any India news is relevant to Telugu people. Score:
- Celebrity name in headline: +8
- Political figure: +6
- Economic pain (petrol, taxes, prices): +5
- AP/Telangana explicit: +4
- Tollywood: +4
- National affecting Telugu: +3
- Threshold for alert: >=10 (not 18)

AUTOMATION: If no spike found in 12 hours, AUTO-PUBLISH anyway.
Scrape best available topic, build video, upload with private premiere.
Then notify Jay via Telegram: "Published X — check and approve at [URL]"

### PHASE 2: Growth (Week 3-8) — "Build the Audience"
Goal: 100+ videos, 500+ subs, first 1000 watch hours

CONTENT SHIFT:
- Add reaction/analysis format: "CM Revanth said X — here's what it means"
- Add comparison videos: "AP vs Telangana — who's doing better?"
- Add "Telugu people react" format to trending national news
- Double down on whatever format gets >5% CTR in analytics

UPLOAD SCHEDULE:
- Main: 9 AM IST + 7 PM IST (two peak hours)
- Shorts: 12 PM + 9 PM IST (lunch + evening scroll time)

COMMUNITY:
- Pin a "welcome" comment on every video asking viewers to subscribe
- Reply to every comment in first 2 hours (YouTube algorithm reward)
- Add end screen pointing to next video

### PHASE 3: Monetization (Month 2-6) — "Cross the Line"
Goal: 1000 subs + 4000 watch hours

WATCH HOURS STRATEGY (this is the real bottleneck):
- 4000 hours = at current 1465 views, need avg watch time of 2.7 min per view
- OR need MORE views: 10,000 views at 24 min avg = 4000 hours
- Focus on RETENTION: hook in first 5 seconds, deliver value, end with cliffhanger

WHAT DRIVES WATCH HOURS:
- Longer videos (15-25 min) with high retention (>50%)
- Playlists that auto-play (news roundup playlist)
- Consistent upload schedule (YouTube recommends reliable channels)
- Clickable thumbnails (face + text + contrast = >5% CTR)

MONETIZATION MATH:
- At 1 sub/day → 1000 subs in ~2.7 years (TOO SLOW)
- Need 5-10 subs/day minimum
- Sub drivers: consistent quality + asking to subscribe + subscriber milestones
- At 50 views/video/day with 2 videos + 4 shorts = 300 views/day
- Need 10% sub conversion = 30 subs/day → 1000 subs in ~33 days
