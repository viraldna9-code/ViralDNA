## Posting Schedule

### X/Twitter (3-5 tweets/day)
- 08:00 IST — Morning news hook (video clip)
- 12:00 IST — Lunch break hot take
- 18:00 IST — Evening analysis thread
- 21:00 IST — Night recap / CTA
- BREAKING — Immediate, any time

### Telegram (1-3 posts/day)
- All new video uploads
- Breaking news alerts
- Exclusive behind-the-scenes clips

### Instagram Reels (1-2/day)
- Repurposed Shorts from YouTube
- 15-30s political commentary clips

## Content Types

### X/Twitter
1. **Video clip + provocative take** — "Here's what [politician] said about [topic] [video]"
2. **Thread breakdown** — "Here's what [event] means for you [thread]"
3. **Quote-tweet news** — Add video analysis to trending political tweets
4. **Poll** — "Who do you think won the debate? A or B?"
5. **CTA** — "Full analysis on our YouTube channel [link]"

### Telegram
1. **Full video posts** — All YouTube videos
2. **Breaking news alerts** — Real-time updates
3. **Exclusive clips** — Behind-the-scenes, extended cuts
4. **Weekly roundup** — Best videos of the week

### Instagram Reels
1. **Repurposed Shorts** — Same content as YouTube Shorts
2. **Quote cards** — Key quotes from politicians with video background
3. **Data visualization** — Election data, budget numbers
