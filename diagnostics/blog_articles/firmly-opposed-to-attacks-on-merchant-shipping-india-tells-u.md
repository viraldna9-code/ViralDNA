## Full Analysis

Attacks on merchant shipping are a big problem, and India just told the UNSC that it s completely against them. This is a crucial stance, especially for a state like Andhra Pradesh, which relies on smooth international trade. Imagine if the goods coming to and from your home state were suddenly at risk that s the kind of impact these attacks can have.

India made its position clear during discussions about the ongoing situation with Iran. These discussions are happening at the United Nations Security Council, which is basically the world s most important meeting place for peace and security. When merchant ships are attacked, it doesn t just affect the ships themselves it can mess with supply chains, make products more expensive, and even slow down economic growth for everyone, including folks in Andhra Pradesh.

So, India speaking up firmly against these attacks is not just a diplomatic statement it s about protecting global trade and, by extension, our own economic well being.
