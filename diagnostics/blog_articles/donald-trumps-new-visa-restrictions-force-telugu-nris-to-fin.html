Big news impacting many Telugu families in America. Former President Donald Trump s new visa rules are making it harder for Indian professionals, especially those from Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, to bring their spouses to the This is leading to a surprising trend many are now looking for marriage partners who already live in America. Traditionally, many Telugu professionals in the would find partners from their home states.

But with the stricter H 4 visa rules, which impact spouses of H 1B visa holders, this is becoming much more difficult. These changes mean that if an H 1B holder marries someone from India, bringing their spouse over could take a very long time, sometimes years, or even be impossible. This uncertainty is causing stress and frustration.

As a result, families are now helping their children in the find partners who are also living there, perhaps on other work visas or green cards. This shift is changing how marriage proposals are handled within the community. It is a direct response to the new immigration policies, forcing a change in long held traditions.
