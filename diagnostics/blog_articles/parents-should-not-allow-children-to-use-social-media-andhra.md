## Full Analysis

Are you letting your kids loose on social media? Well, a big warning is coming out of Andhra Pradesh. The state s child rights panel is saying parents should seriously think twice about letting their children use social media at all.

They ve even sent a note to the Minister for Education, Nara Lokesh, suggesting a new law. Imagine a law just for protecting kids online from all sorts of adult content. This is like putting up a super strong fence around your child s digital world.

The panel wants to study if this is possible. Andhra Pradesh, with all its development and its global Telugu family, is looking at how to keep its youngest safe in this digital age. Think of it like parents teaching kids to look both ways before crossing the street, but for the internet.
