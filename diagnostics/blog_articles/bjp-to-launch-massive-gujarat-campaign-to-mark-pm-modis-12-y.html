Did you know a major political event is set to kick off in Gujarat very soon? This widespread effort will cover all districts, significant milestone in Indian politics.

The campaign aims to highlight the various achievements and developmental projects undertaken during PM Modi s long service, first as Chief Minister of Gujarat and then as Prime Minister of India. Party leaders and workers will engage with citizens, sharing stories of progress and positive change that have touched many lives. This initiative is not just a celebration, but also a way to connect with the public and reinforce the party s further development.

It s expected to involve numerous public gatherings, outreach programs, and community events designed to reach a broad audience.
